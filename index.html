What is the issue?

Education helps people learn skills, get jobs and make safe choices. Some communities — especially Indigenous or remote groups — face big barriers.

Why it matters

  • Better education → more job opportunities
  • Improves health and safety
  • Girls and Indigenous students face extra challenges

Barriers

  • No nearby school or transport
  • Not enough teachers or materials
  • Lessons not linked to culture

Case Study: Australia (Aboriginal Communities)

Remote Aboriginal communities often have fewer schooling resources. Distance, culture and attendance barriers affect learning outcomes.

Clontarf Foundation supports Aboriginal boys through mentoring and sport.
